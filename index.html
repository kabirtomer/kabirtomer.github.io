About

I am a third-year PhD student at UIUC, advised by Dakshita Khurana. Previously, I was an undergraduate at IIT Delhi advised by Venkata Koppula. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science. My research focuses on questions at the intersection of quantum computing and cryptography.

Some of my work was recently featured in a Quanta Magazine article.
